About

Topsy's Cuisine

Topsy's Cuisine is already abuzz with an exellent reputation for delivering fine foods and outstanding.

Our experienced staff come with great strength in health and hygiene, and we are well used to dealing with large, high profile events, where preparation and careful attention to detail, really matter.

We use nothing but the freshest ingredients and we maintain street quality control by producing more than 90% of all our food, in-house.

Only specialist products are sourced externally and in such cases only the finest suppliers are selected.

We have built our reputation and dependability and our willinginess to deliver exactly what the customer wants everytime. That means we are perfectly happy to work with you to produce a tailor made menu, where the emphasis might be on healthy eating, local produce or any number of special dietry requirements.

Recipes

Chicken Pita

  1. Fry your chicken with olive oil until chicken is white (season with Rajah Chicken Spice).
  2. On a seperate pan fry your vegetables with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. To make your pita bread, mix flour, salt and water together in a bowl, until the dough is smooth. Afterwards pick small balls from your dough and roll them out to make a VERY flat circle.
  4. Fry your pita circles in a clean pan (DO NOT add oil)
